About

Corey Lee’s flagship restaurant remains a serene oasis in SoMa, where diners are treated to a masterclass in culinary precision. The tasting menu seamlessly weaves Asian techniques and flavors with Western fine-dining sensibilities. Signatures like the faux shark fin soup and thousand-year-old quail egg are enduring testaments to a kitchen operating at the highest level of execution and creativity.
