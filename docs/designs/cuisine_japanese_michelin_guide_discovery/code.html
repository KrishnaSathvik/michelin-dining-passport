Terminology

Omakase
"I'll leave it up to you." A meal consisting of dishes selected by the chef.
Kaiseki
A traditional multi-course Japanese dinner; the ultimate expression of seasonal dining.
Edomae
Traditional Tokyo-style sushi, often involving marinating or curing the fish.
